Enrollment Scholarships

The Rio Summer School of Informatics offers students registration scholarships for different courses. The beneficiaries of these may attend the courses without any charge.

The requirement to apply for the scholarship is as follows:

  • Be enrolled in a degree or postgraduate course at a public university in Argentina. In the case of being enrolled in a degree course, you must have passed at least one subject.
